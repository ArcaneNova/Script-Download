**Key Features:**
- ✅ Forward from restricted channels
- ✅ Accept message links (don't need forwarding)
- ✅ Copy to multiple target channels
- ✅ Works with bot accounts or user accounts
- ✅ Shows real-time progress
- ✅ No limits on message count
- ✅ Works 24/7
